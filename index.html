About

Survey Services

Grain Cargo

Inspection, sampling and supervision of grain cargoes, including wheat, barley, corn and other bulk agricultural commodities, in accordance with GAFTA rules.

Vegetable Oils

Inspections, ullages of the ship's tanks and shore tanks, sampling and supervision of vegetable oils, including sunflower seed oil, rapeseed oil, olive oil, palm oil, palm olein, palm stearin, coconut oil and other edible oils, in accordance with FOSFA rules.

Petroleum Products

Quantity measurements, sampling and inspections of petroleum products, including diesel, gasoline and other refined products, in accordance with API / ASTM standards.

Steel Products

Pre-shipment inspections, loading supervision, stowage control and lashing inspections.

Tally Count

Organization of tally count for steel products, bagged cargoes and general cargoes.

Cargo Holds Inspections

Inspections of cargo holds for cleanliness and suitability for loading of the intended bulk cargo.

Cargo Tanks Inspections

Inspections of cargo tanks for cleanliness and suitability for loading of the intended liquid cargo.

Hatch Covers Inspections

Inspections of vessels weather deck openings for visual apparent condition and ultrasonic testing for watertight integrity.

Draught Survey

Calculations of loaded or discharged cargo quantity by draught survey.

Sealing of Hatch Covers

Sealing of hatch covers in order to protect the shipowners and cargo interests against shortage-related risks.

Bunker Survey

Calculation of bunker quantities on board the vessel, on-hire bunker survey, off-hire bunker survey, bunker survey during bunkering operations along with taking samples of bunkered fuel oil or diesel oil, bunker-related investigations.

On-Hire Condition Survey

On-hire ispections of the vessels on delivery of into charter or off-hire inspections on re-delivery back to owners.

Stock Inspections

Inspections of various kinds of cargoes at the storage facilities and warehouses in order to confirm the presence, apparent condition and declared quantities.

Monitorings

Monitoring of loading and discharging operations of bulk cargoes, including grains, fertilisers, sulphur, iron ore concentrate, iron ore pellets, etc., for compliance with transshipment conditions

Project Cargo Inspections

Inspection and supervision of project cargo, including wind turbine components, vehicles and trucks, heavy machinery, packaged cargoes for visual apparent condition, and compliance with lashing and securing requirements.

Perishable Cargoes

Inspections of perishable cargoes including bananas, fruits, fish, meat and other frozen products shipped in containers.

Ships Condition Surveys

Condition surveys of vessels on behalf of P&I Clubs, insurers, owners or charterers in accordance with the recommended Clubs forms or with the agreed check list.

Damage Surveys

Damage surveys, collecting evidences, investigations of maritime incidents, including collisions, cargo damage, oil spills, groundings, ballast water issues and damage to shore facilities.

Web Tools

Some inspections can be supported by simple web tools developed to assist surveyors with notes, calculations and reporting.

Draught Survey Web App

Open Web App

About

This web application is designed for ships officers and marine surveyors to perform accurate draught survey calculations.

It allows you to keep your data securely in the cloud storage and perform surveys across multiple devices.

Features

Create, edit, delete, and keep your inspections in cloud storage.

Keep in the cloud main vessel information (LBP, Light Ship, distances draught marks from PP, etc.), and automatically reuse it for the future inpections.

Autofill the hydrostatic data based on previous inspections or saved enties.

Add and update your notes during inspection.

Export results to Excel file for more convenient reporting, printing, or further editing, using ready-to-use public templates.

Trucks Weighing Web App

Open Web App

About

Trucks Weighing Web App is a web application for cargo surveyors and tally teams that helps control cargo weight during truck operations at terminals - in ports, warehouses, and silos (loading or discharging).

Your data is stored securely in the database and can be accessed from multiple devices for team workflows.

Features

Two control points: weighbridge and at berth (cargo operations).

Truck statuses: the operator at weighbridge point can see at glance the status of all trucks - after initial weighing, under cargo operations, arrival at the cargo operations area, cargo operations started/finished, and after final weighing.

Safety logic: the operator at the cargo operations point can verify, which trucks have passed the initial weighing and are ready for cargo operations.

Extended team workflow: multi-user concurrent input.

Notes: add and update your notes during inspection, notes are visible to the tally team.

Plate number autocomplete: based on previously entered values.

Logo: select a logo on the inspection form, it is printed in the PDF header. Up to 5 logos per account.

PDF export: standard daily and summary “Tally Sheet”, totals and balance, optional period filtering.
