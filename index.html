About

Survey Services

Grain Cargo

Inspection, sampling and supervision of grain cargoes, including wheat, barley, corn and other bulk agricultural commodities in accordance with GAFTA rules.

Vegetable Oils

Inspections, ullages of the ship's tanks and shore tanks, sampling and supervision of vegetable oils, including sunflower seed oil, rapeseed oil, olive oil, palm oil, palm olein, palm stearin, coconut oil and other edible oils in accordance with FOSFA rules.

Petroleum Products

Quantity measurements, sampling and inspections of petroleum products, including diesel, gasoline and other refined products in accordance with API / ASTM standards.

Steel Products

Pre-shipment inspections, loading supervision, stowage control and lashing inspections.

Tally Count

Organization and supervision of tally count for steel products, bagged cargoes and general cargoes.

Cargo Holds Inspections

Inspections of cargo holds for cleanliness and suitability prior to loading of the intended bulk cargo.

Cargo Tanks Inspections

Inspections of cargo tanks for cleanliness and suitability prior to loading of the intended liquid cargo.

Hatch Covers Inspections

Visual inspections, chalk testing and ultrasonic testing for watertight integrity.

Draught Survey

Calculations of loaded or discharged cargo quantity by draught survey.

Sealing of Hatch Covers

Sealing of hatch covers in order to protect the shipowners' and cargo interests against shortage-related risks.

Bunker Survey

Calculation of bunker quantities on board the vessel, on-hire bunker survey, off-hire bunker survey, bunker survey during bunkering operations, bunker-related investigations.

On-Hire Condition Survey

Visual inspections of the vessels during on-hire condition survey inspection on delivery of vessel into charter or off-hire inspection on redelivery back to owners.

Stock Inspections

Inspections of various kinds of cargoes at the storage facilities and warehouses in order to confirm the presence and apparent condition of declared quantities.

Monitorings

Monitoring of loading and discharging operations of bulk cargoes, including grains, fertilisers, sulphur, iron ore concentrate, iron ore pellets, etc.

Project Cargo Inspections

Inspection and supervision of project cargo, including wind turbine components, vehicles and trucks, heavy machinery, packaged cargoes.

Perishable Cargoes

Inspections of perishable cargoes including bananas, fruits and frozen products shipped in containers.

Ship’s Condition Surveys

Condition surveys of vessels on behalf of P&I Clubs, insurers, owners or charterers in accordance with the agreed scope of inspection.

Damage Surveys

Damage surveys, collecting evidences, investigations of maritime incidents, including collisions, cargo damage, oil spills, groundings, ballast water issues and damage to shore facilities.
